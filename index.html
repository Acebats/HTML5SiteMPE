Welcome to the Mako Precision Engineering site.

Mako Precision Engineering is an ISO 9001 registered firm based in Bilston in the heart of the area known as the Black Country, famous for it's manufacturing heritage.

Mako Precision was formed by a group of skilled engineers who for many years worked for a very successful precision engineering company. The team that provided the driving force behind this success, now own and operate Mako Precision Engineering.

Mako Precision Engineering constantly strive to ensure that all of our manufactured parts, procedures and assemblies consistently match or exceed our customer expectations and requirements. All staff are dedicated to providing you, our valued customer, with the service, support and quality you require.

Our experienced and highly skilled CNC engineers have the confidence and technical knowledge to work to the tightest of tolerances, regardless of the job specification. They have the flexibility to use traditional programming techniques (i.e. point to point and parametric programming at the machine panel) or employ the latest DNC linked computer systems and software, giving us the option of producing programs using the quickest methods possible, which puts us in the best position for a quicker turnaround and a more cost effective product. We as a team get great satisfaction from providing our customers with a value for money, quality product.

Our team has the skills required to allow us to povide services to a wide variety of market sectors, such as the following:

Toolmaking

Mako Precision Engineering are able to prove a complete toolmaking service to the toolmaker. We have expertise in designing and manufacturing complete tools or producing complex die components for toolmakers as part of larger projects

Agricultural

Mako Precision Engineering have produced many components for the agricultural sector, including bearing housings, bushes, shafts and sprockets, plus various add-on components for agricultural equipment. Mako Precision also produce new and spare parts for grass cutting machinery.

Motorsport

Mako Precision Engineering have produced many components for the motorsport industry and provide a very quick turnaround at a good price.

Automotive

In addition to our services for the motorsport sector, Mako Precision Engineering also manufacture fixtures, tools and parts for the automotive sector in general. Above is a checking fixture we manufactured for a turbo core

Hydraulics

Mako Precision Engineering have years of experience in providing components for the hydraulics industry. We have produced components for hydraulics which are currently being used across the United Kingdom.

Patternmaking

Mako Precision Engineering make items for patternmaking for the foundry industry

Rolling Mills

Mako Precision Engineering has a specialism in the rolling mills sector, providing parts for both hot and cold rolling mills.

Services

Mako Precision offers a range of precision engineering services including:

CNC Milling

As with our CNC turning service, our CNC machining centres offer the same quality and flexibility, plus the ability to machine complex 3D and 2D forms to your specification, often costing many times less than similar components, that in the past would have been produced using manual machining techniques. Complex surface programs can be created from a CAD drawing and be "drip fed" to the machine via a DNC link, saving many hours in programming time.

CNC Turning

Mako Precision offers a comprehensive CNC turning service, working from customer provided CAD data, existing samples or prototype, or often just a simple sketch, CNC turning offers unprecedented product consistency and repeatability, giving excellent quality and accuracy throughout an entire batch of components.
